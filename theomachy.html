Core Features

Everything to build
your legend

Three powerful pillars power the THEOMACHY experience — from creation to combat.

🧬
Hero Forge
A deep customization engine. Pick from elemental, cosmic, tech, or biological power trees. Combine multiple abilities and shape your hero's appearance, backstory, and moral code.
🏛️
Squad Assembly
Invite real users or AI-controlled heroes into your squad. Assign roles, build synergies between powers, and develop team strategies in the pre-battle war room.
🌍
The Arena
Dynamic battle environments — cities, space, underworld — where squads clash in real-time. Crowd influence, collateral damage, and public trust all affect battle outcomes.
📖
Living Lore
Every battle writes history. A shared universe timeline records alliances, betrayals, and legendary moments — shaping who gets labeled hero or villain by society.
🎯
Threat Intelligence
Identify rogue heroes posing threats to society. Coordinate intel, plan counter-measures, and issue public threat alerts before launching defensive operations.
🏆
Legacy System
Heroes earn reputations, titles, and power upgrades based on battle history and public perception. Your legacy evolves across every fight.

Developer's Vision

The pitch

Additya Pramanik

Why THEOMACHY, and why now?

The gaming and social entertainment space is saturated with games where you play a hero someone else imagined. THEOMACHY flips this entirely — it hands the pen to the user. In a world where personalization and co-creation drive engagement, there is an enormous untapped market for a platform that merges deep RPG-style hero creation with real-time multiplayer battle strategy.

What makes THEOMACHY technically compelling is its modular power system — a graph-based ability engine where powers combine, clash, or amplify each other dynamically. Pair this with a shared universe persistence layer and real-time squad coordination, and we have a product that is not just a game — it's a platform.

From a business perspective, the monetization vectors are clear: hero cosmetics, squad expansions, seasonal lore chapters, and a creator marketplace. The community loop is self-reinforcing — every new hero added enriches the universe for every other player.

This is the right moment. The technology is ready. The audience is hungry. All we need is the will to build it.
